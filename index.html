The Science Behind Reaction Time

Reaction time is the interval between the presentation of a stimulus and the initiation of a response. According to research:

  • The average human reaction time to visual stimuli is about 250 milliseconds
  • Professional athletes often achieve reaction times under 200ms
  • Regular practice can improve reaction time by 15-20%

Our game is designed based on principles from cognitive psychology and sports science to provide effective training.
